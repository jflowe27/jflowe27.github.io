Project Overview

The application I plan to develop is a website to showcase artwork by Ronnie Taylor. The website will display images and videos of various projects they have worked on, including a list of future projects and a contact form.

Intended Users

  • Art enthusiasts and anyone who appreciates art
  • Friends, fans, and supporters
  • General public and students
  • The artist themself, as a digital portfolio

Content

  • Home -- Overview of the website and brief explanation
  • Gallery -- Showcase of all of the artwork, separated into pages for different forms
  • About -- Biography of the artist and their inspirations
  • Contact -- Inquiry form

Site Map

Page Design

Home

  • Purpose: To welcome visitors, introduce the artist, and present a quick overfiew of the website's purpose.
  • Audience: General visitors, art fans, students, and potential collaborators.
  • Content: A hero image or featured artwork, short introduction text, navigation links, and a preview of the gallery.
  • Data Entry: No, this page does not collect user data.
  • Buttons/Links: Navigation bar menu items / a "View Gallery" button.
  • Actions: Buttons to navigate to other pages
  • Future JS: Interactive featured artwork carousel using jQuery and a fade animation effect.

Gallery

  • Purpose: To showcase the artist's work in an organized, visually engaging layout.
  • Audience: Art enthusiasts, friends, general public, curators.
  • Content: Artwork images grouped into categories such as paintings, crochet, and jewelry. Each artwork includes a title and short description.
  • Data Entry: No, users will not enter data here.
  • Buttons/Links: Category buttons or tabs, clickable images opening in a lightbox.
  • Actions: Users can filter art by category, open artwork in expanded view, or scroll through collections.
  • Future JS: jQuery-UI tabs for categories, image lightbox plugin, and AJAX loading of artwork data from a JSON file.

About the Artist

  • Purpose: To share the artist's background, influences, and creative processes.
  • Audience: Anyone who wants to learn more about the artist, potential collaborators, and art students.
  • Content: A biography section, artist photo, list of inspirations, and possibly quotes or artist statements.
  • Data Entry: No, this page does not collect information.
  • Buttons/Links: Links to gallery, contact page, or social media.
  • Actions: Navigation only, no interactive content beyond reading.
  • Future JS: Accordion UI widget for "Bio / Inspirations / Techniques" using jQuery-UI.

Contact

  • Purpose: To allow users to send inquiries, feedback, or commission requests to the artist.
  • Audience: Clients, collaborators, and interested viewers.
  • Content: A contact form with fields such as name, email, message, and reason for inquiry.
  • Data Entry: Yes, users enter information into a form.
  • Buttons/Links: Submit button, optional reset button
  • Actions: Form uses JavaScript to validate fields and then shows a confirmation message.
  • Future JS: Form validation with jQuery, confirmation message.

Dynamic Functionality

The website will include several forms of interactivity to enhance the user experience. These features will be implemented using JavaScript, jQuery, jQuery-UI, and simple AJAX.

  • Image Lightbox
  • Category Filters
  • Featured Artwork Slider
  • Interactive Accordion
  • Contact Form Validation
  • AJAX Artwork Loading

Similar examples of interactivity can be seen on these websites:

  • https://lokeshdhakar.com/projects/lightbox2/
  • https://jqueryui.com/tabs/
  • https://jqueryui.com/accordion/
