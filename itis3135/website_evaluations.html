Website Evaluations

Spotify

Spotify is a app that allows users to listen to music, podcasts, and audiobooks.

  • Contrast: This site has dark backgrounds with soft white text and bright green highlights.
  • Repetition: Styles and sizes of both fonts and images are consistent throughout the entire site.
  • Alignment: There are multiple resizable and collapsable windows, where content is all left-aligned. Navigation is at the top.
  • Proximity: Album/Artist pictures, names, and descriptions are grouped together to show relation, while separating those groups from unrelated sections.

Yale School of Art

The webpage for Yale School of Art is a collaborative experiment page that allows all members of the community to edit the website.

  • Contrast: Background is a repeating image with a lot going on, distracting from the main content of the page. Additionally, in several places, text is hard to read due to font and background color.
  • Repetition: Numerous font styles and sizes, nearly every section has a different background color, and the background of the page is the same image of a chair repeated three times.
  • Alignment: (Almost) everything is left-aligned with considerable space to the right of the page, causing uneven distribution.
  • Proximity: In some sections, unrelated content is grouped together, while in others related content is not grouped together, causing confusion as to what goes with what.
